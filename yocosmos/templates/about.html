About Page

Welcome to Yo Cosmos

Yo Cosmos is an alternative to traditional social media platforms.

On Yo Cosmos there are no accounts, no usernames, no profiles, and no images. Any user can yeet their thoughts out into the cosmos as often as they choose (within reasonable rate limits).

What is Yo Cosmos?

Have something to say, but no one to say it to? Want something to be known to the universe?

Yo Cosmos is the perfect place to hurl your thoughts, coherent or otherwise, into the Cosmos. (Who knows, maybe you'll hear back...)

Cosmos Code of Conduct

Yo Cosmos is a text-only message board. We have rules of conduct that all users must obey at all times. By using this website, you agree to abide by the rules and terms set forth on this page, in our Terms of Service, and in our Privacy Policy.

We reserve the right to change or modify these rules at any time without prior notice or reason.

Rules

  1. Don't post excessively inappropriate content

    2. Any posts containing content deemed excessively inappropriate by the site administrators (including self censored, automatically censored, acronyms and slang) will be removed from the forum at the discretion of the administrators. Posts containing pornography or illegal content will be removed and the user's ip adderss banned.

  2. Be tolerant

    3. Racial, sexual, or any other form of discrimination is not tolerated. Posts that contain discriminatory content will be removed at the discretion of the site administrators.

  3. Don't doxx anyone (including yourself)

    4. Don't post personally identifiable information (i.e. name, address, email, phone number, etc.), without explicit prior consent of all parties involved. Personally identifiable information that is posted with clear malicious intent will be removed by the administrators.

  4. Don't advertise

    5. Do not use Yo Cosmos to generate traffic or attention for your endeavor, links to personal websites and projects are permitted within reason as determined by the site administrators. Commercial activities of any kind are not permitted on Yo Cosmos. Any exception to these rules must be pre-authorised on a case by case basis by an administrator.

  5. Don't behave in a malicious manner

    6. Do not behave in a manner that can cause distress, harm or inconvenience to other members, staff or the site itself. This includes, but is not limited to, discrimination, bullying, offensive material, and breaching others privacy.

  6. Don't engage in illegal activity

    7. Yocosmos.com is located in the United States, and as such, United States law must be followed at all times. This includes Copyright law and Computer Misuse law (Malware, Virus etc).

  7. Reporting content

    8. Please do not make posts to inform other users of site rules or attempt to enforce rules without the authorization of site administrators. Use the Contact link at the bottom of the page to inform administrators when a post breaks the website rules or when administrator attention is needed.
